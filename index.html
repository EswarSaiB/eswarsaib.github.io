Who I Am

I'm Eswar Sai Bhallamudi, a Full Stack Developer based in Hyderabad, India with 8+ years of professional experience in the IT and services industry. My core stack is .NET / C# on the backend paired with Angular on the frontend, all connected via well-designed REST APIs and backed by SQL Server.
